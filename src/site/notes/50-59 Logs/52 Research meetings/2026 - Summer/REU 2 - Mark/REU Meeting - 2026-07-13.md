This following is a brief summary of our research meeting on 2026-07-13.

## Meeting summary
---

We discussed the categorical definition of trace, which required more lead-up than it might sound. First we talked about enriched categories and 2-categories, including many examples in the Kapranov paper. Then we slowly worked through the idea of 2-arrows from a identity 1-arrow to another 1-arrow, finally leading to a functor ${\bf Tr}:\operatorname{Hom}_C(1_x,A)\to {\bf Set}$ .

## Tasks for next meeting
---

Let's continue to tackle the papers by Ponto-Shulman and Kapranov-Ganter until we get a better feel for the categorical characteristic.
