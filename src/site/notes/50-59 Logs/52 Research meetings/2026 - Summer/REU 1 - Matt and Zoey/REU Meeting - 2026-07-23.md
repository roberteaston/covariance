This following is a brief summary of our research meeting on 2026-07-23.

## Meeting summary
---

We went over the constructions and properties of both the free Lie algebra functor (from ${\bf Vec}_k\to {\bf LieAlg}_k$) and universal enveloping algebra functor (from ${\bf LieAlg}_k\to {\bf AssocAlg}_k$). There are/were way too many details to check, but the main constructions involved using the tensor product (and basically the tensor algebra), and taking quotients by appropriate ideals.
