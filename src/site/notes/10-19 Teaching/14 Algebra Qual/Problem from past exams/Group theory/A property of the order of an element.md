Let $G$ be a group and $a\in G$ be an element. Let $n\in {\bf N}$ be the smallest positive number such that $a^n=e$, where $e$ is the identity element. Show that the set

$$\{e,a,a^2,\ldots, a^{n-1}\}$$

contains no repetitions.
