Let $G$ be a group and $H\leq G$ a subgroup. For each coset $aH$ of $H$ in $G$, define the set

$$G_{aH}=\{b\in G\,|\,baH=aH\}.$$

1. Prove that $G_{aH}$ is a subgroup of $G$.
2. Suppose that $H$ is normal in $G$. Prove that $G_{aH}=H$.
