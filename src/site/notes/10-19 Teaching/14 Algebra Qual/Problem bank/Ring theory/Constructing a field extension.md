Let $f(x)=x^3+x+1\in {\bf Z}_5[x]$.

1. Prove that $f(x)$ is irreducible.
2. Prove that $\langle f(x)\rangle$ is a maximal ideal.
3. What is the cardinality of ${\bf Z}_5[x]/\langle f(x)\rangle$? Justify.
