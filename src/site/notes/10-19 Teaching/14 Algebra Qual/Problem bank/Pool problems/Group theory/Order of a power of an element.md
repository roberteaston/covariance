Suppose $G$ is a cyclic group of order $n$, and $t\in G$ is a generator.

1. Give a positive integer $d$ such that $t^{-1}=t^d$.
2. Let $c$ be an integer and let $m=\gcd(n,c)$. Prove that the order of $t^c$ is $\frac{n}{m}$.
