Let $G$ be a finite group and $\operatorname{Z}(G)$ denote its center.

1. Prove that if $G/\operatorname{Z}(G)$ is cyclic, then $G$ is abelian.
2. Prove that if $G$ is nonabelian, then $|\operatorname{Z}(G)|\leq \frac{1}{4}|G|$.
