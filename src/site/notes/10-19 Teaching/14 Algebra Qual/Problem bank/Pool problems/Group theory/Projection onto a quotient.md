Suppose $G$ is a group, $H$ and $K$ are normal subgroups of $G$, and $H\leq K$.

1. Define a group homomorphism from $K$ to $G/H$.
2. Compute the kernel of the homomorphism in (a), and apply the First Isomorphism Theorem.
