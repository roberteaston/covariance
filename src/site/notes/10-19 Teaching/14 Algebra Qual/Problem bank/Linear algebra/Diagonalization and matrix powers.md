Let $A=\begin{bmatrix} 0 & 0 & -2 \\ 1 & 2 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 & 3\end{bmatrix}$.

1. Determine whether $A$ is diagonalizable, and if so, give its diagonal form along with a diagonalizing matrix.
2. Compute $A^{42}$. Remember to show all work.
