Let $A=\begin{bmatrix} -2 & 1 & -1 \\ 5 & -2 & 2 \\ 7 & -3 & 3\end{bmatrix}$.

1. Find the characteristic polynomial and the minimal polynomial of $A$.
2. Find the Jordan canonical form of the matrix $A$.
